Ur  iyi-ixuṣṣ  wara  lḥamdu  li  Llah,  seg  wasmi  i  d-nekker  yella lxir,  tella  lxedma,  ddwa  yella,  ameɣbun  yeṛwa  (Ad ibedel amkan).  Ma  d  amarkanti  yettḥewwis  ɣɣaren  warraw-is  meskin yettuɣaḍ  yeṛwa  lḥif.  Le fromage crevette les fruits exotiques yectaq  meskin abazin abumexluḍ,  aɣrum  aquran.  Yekkat  kan  lbuftik,  lkabyar,  lḥut,  a mačči  am  nekni  a  madam  lǧuǧa  la  t-nettwali  kan  di  tilivizyu, axaṭer tilivizyu-nneɣ taqqar-d kan ṣṣeḥ d ayen kan i ɣ-d-zzkanen 
(Ad d-yezzi ɣer yimeshanayen )  Aha  ziɣ  anwa  deg-wen  i ixuṣṣen  neɣ  i  yenxeṣṣen,  anwa,  aha  beggen-d  iman-ik  ad  k- waliɣ,  mmuten  merra  a  Madame  Lǧuǧa,  yaxi  taṛwam  merra, twalaḍ ulac wi ara d-ineṭqen... Wa ssukutu yadullu ɛala rriḍa. timḥemmest,
