Amek ad ḥemmlen warrac taɣuri? 

Asegmi. Ulac ababat ara d-yini ur sɛiɣ ara ugur n uwelleh i tɣuri akked mmi 
ameẓyan. Llan kra n warrac ttagin s yimeṭṭi ed tussda tisin ɣer uɣerbaz deg useggas 
amenzu. Anect yebɣu iban-d dakken leqraya d tiwaculin, maca ula d asegmi yesɛa 
azal d agaɛmir ed nnuba tazaɛlukt bac ad yerr tarwa i ubrid. 
Acku  taggara-a,  ula  d  inelmaden  iqefzen 
ḍḥan-d  ɛuffen  abdar  n  wawal  «taɣuri», 
ɛyan deg-s ad as-tiniḍ mɣuren yakan yes-s 
nutni  mazal-iten  d  igerdan.  Ɣef  waya, 
asteqsi d wa: acimi ɛuffen warrac taɣuri?  
Seg  yiwet  n  tama,  nezmer  ad  nesseḍlem 
tarrayt n uselmed, imi ur temmug s waṭas 
n leḥsabat. Ahat yewwi-d ad yili uɛiwed n 
tmuɣli deg wahil n usseqri. Ama d ahil s 
timmad-is, neɣ d aqeɛɛed-ines deg wakud 
akked laɛmer n unelmad. Tal tamsirt yes-
sefk ad temmezg i umdur n unelmad. Rnu 
ɣer-s, iban-d dakken segmi uɣalen wussan 
n  ustaɛfu  d  sin  (lǧemɛa  ed  ssebt),  yeḥres 
cwiṭ wahil n ddurt war ma yefka-d agum-
mu  d  ugemmuḍ.  Segmi  yeɣzif    usgunfu, 
anelmad  yegza-tt  d  astaɛfu  s  unamek 
asemdan. Meḥsub, ussan n sem ed sed ur 
yebɣa  ara  ad  imuqel  ulama  d  tamuɣli  i 
uqrab. Yesɛeddiw akud n sin n wussan deg 
turart, alama qrib yennum acali ulac taɣu-
ri,  dɣa  ad  yefqaɛ  mara  d-yaweḍ  acer 
(lḥed).  Acimi  ḥemmlen  ad  tili  tagant 
(greve)  yal  mraw  neɣ  snat  n  tmerwin  n 
wussan,  lemmer  mačči  d  amrar  i  ten-ye-
qqnen  akked  tɣuri  i  igezmen.  Lemmer 
mačči ulama d atentuf n tayri ur tt-sɛan i 
tɣuri. Ɣas ma yella, ameqqran dima yesse-
gzay-as-d  i  umecṭuḥ  dakken  ala  taɣuri  i 
izemren  ad  t-yenfaɛ,  ladɣa  deg  tmurt-a 
deg nella. 
Imawlan ad gen ayen yellan fell-asen 
Deg  waddad-a  yettwabḍan  akked  yi-
mawlan merra, ilaq xersum yiwen ad yeg 
seg  tama-s  allalen  akk  iwulmen  i  tɣuri 
igerrzen  n  mmi-s. Acku  ma  yella-d  netta 
deffir n uɛuffu i iɛuf mmi-s leqraya, dinna 
d tawaɣit. Seg dduzan n tɣuri, ɣer yiselsa 
ed  tgella  (ma  ur  itett  ara  deg  usečču  n 
uɣerbaz), anelmad ur ilaq akk ad d-yaf ta-
mentilt  (sebba)  seg  tama  n  yimawlan-is. 
Akkenni, ɣas ad igerrez wahil, ɣas ad ifaz 
uselmad  daɣen,  wanag  lebɣi  i  tɣuri  d 
awezɣi  ad  d-yeḥnunneḍ  i  unelmad  ma 
yeẓra  belli  txus-it  tɣawsa,  ma  yettwali 
amdakkel-is yesɛa kraḍ n yimruyen netta 
uhu. Tasuta-a ur tennum ara lḥuj am wak-

ken  i  d-kkren  yibabaten-nneɣ.  Lamaɛna 
ɣas akken, ɣran gzan. Tella taluft nniḍen, 
tin  n  tigzi  seg  tama  n  yimawlan  daɣen, 
axaṭer agrud d agrud. Ihi yezmer yiwen ad 
as-d-yexdem ul n tɣuri i mmi-s s kra n tɣa-
riwin. Mara ad as-yeṛgem taɣawsa i iḥem-
mel ma yewwi-d tazmilt neɣ talemmast n 
lɛali. Anect-a ad yedḥes ugar anelmad ak-
ken ad iɣer war ma iḥulfa d kra n uḥettem 
neɣ n uqezzul i yemuggen fell-as. Ihi d ta 
daɣen i d nnuba tagejdant n yimawlan. Ma 
ssnen  abrid  n  tikli  i  mmi-tsen  s  tegzi  ed 
leḥnana, akken yebɣu yili ad ssiwḍen i kra 
seg wayen ttraǧun.
 
Taẓayt n uqrab d aɣilif nniḍen n warrac 
Ahat drus i yeẓran dakken taẓayt n uqrab d 
ugur  idumen  n  uqcic,  skud  aneggaru-a  d 
agrud, ur yeẓra acu yellan s tidet, ur d-ye-
qqar ma yella ayen yettawi i uɣerbaz-is d 
abuqil  iɛetteb-it,  (skud  yemmend).  Maca 
am  wakken  nettwali  ed  wamek  i  d-nnan 
yimejjayen, aqrab n tɣuri yezmer ad yili d 
amihi ameqqran n unelmad ɣef tdawsa-s. 

Ass-a,  deg  tesmilin  tineggura  n  uɣerbaz 
amenzu  (tis  4,  tis  5)  akked  tmezwura  n 
uɣerbaz  alemmas  (tamenzut  ed  tis-snat), 
aqrab n unelmad yettaweḍ almi n 8 n yiki-
luyen - gar dduzan ed uqrab - seg wakken 
yeḥres wahil. D aya ihi ay d aɣbel n uqcic. 
Aqrab yeẓẓayen i yezgan ɣef tuyat n ugrud 
yezmer ad t-iḍur yerna aṭas. Ayen ara tent-
yaɣen deg-s srid d ajgu n tammast-is. Sim-
mal yettɛebbi-as abuqil i ur nlaq, simmal 
ad yettmil ujgu n tammast-is I tama akken 
ad as-d-yeggri d aṭṭan deg yimal. Tamehla 
n  yal  aɣerbaz  ihi  yessulef  ad  terr  awel-
lih-is i taluft-a bac ur ttenṭerrayen aṭas yi-
gerdan-nneɣ.  Deg  wannim-a  merra, 
nezmer ad nẓer dacu iregglen abrid i war-
rac-nneɣ,  imi  aqqa  amenzu,  ilaq-asen 
ttawil send takkit.
