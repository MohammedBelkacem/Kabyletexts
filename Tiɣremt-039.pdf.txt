Akken  nessen  d  waken  neẓra, 

Lyaban d tamurt iqerben tamurt 
n  Cinwa,  ansa  i  d-ilul  waṭṭan-a 
isemremden  imezdaɣ  akk  umaḍal,

Akken i ɣ-disefhem Uzzayri-ya yedd-

kra  ixulfen  gar  tudert  n  zik  d  tin  seg

Lxilaf d tmura niḍen yezga deg 
tterbiya.
Tugett n tmura deg ddunit, akken nwa-

n  diri,  am  yinebran  d  umennuɣ 
sbaḥ-meddi gar warrac d yimawlan, i 
d-yekkan  seg  tikti-ya  n  ukman.  Gar 
tmura-ya  llant  tid  i  tt-yeččan  deg  te-
dyant-a  n  waṭṭan  COVID-19,  llant  ti 
dur  yettwaḍurren  ara.  Maca,  deg 
Lyaban, lḥukuma tessuter deg yimez-
daɣ-is,  ad  sxedmen  laɛqel  d  lmeɛqul 
deg  lecɣal-a,  aɛni  win  iḥulfan  s  yi-

dɣa ad yeslek netta ad yernu ur isehlak 
wid  i  s-d-yezzin.  S  wawal  kan,  med-
den akk uɣen rray. Ulac la lqanun ara 
iḥettmen yiwen la iɣallen n laman.

Ilaq ad nɛiwen, ad d-nin belli imezdaɣ 
n tmurt n Lyaban ttwassnen s lemqa-
dra-nsen n lqanun d lecɣal n talsa, ak-
ken tebɣu tili liḥala. D alag i d-yettgen 
lxilaf gar-asen d lbaqi n yemdanen deg 
umaḍal.
ɣef nihwan i d-tefka lḥukuma n Lyaban 
i yimezdaɣ-is, d wid i d-yettmeslayen 
ɣef  lecɣal ur  neffiɣ  i  lɛada n  Lyaban, 
aɛni asizdeg. « Da, imezdaɣ n tmurt n 
Lyaban  ttḥadaren  iman-nsen  s  tezdeg 
am ’zal am yiḍ, d tekmamin ur ntekkes 
alamma  iɛedda  fell-as  lawan.  Akken
